My Hobbies

I enjoy doing many different things and having new experiences so I try to have a lot of fun hobbies. This page will be dedicated to the certain activities I entertain myself with and that are my go to for fun. Below is a list of my favorite activities and I will be going in depth to each one of them!

Hobbies list:

  • Soccer
  • Surfing
  • Painting
  • Reading Tarot Cards

Soccer

I started playing soccer when I was in elemntary school and instantly knew it was something I was going to stick with for as long as I could. Its the only time I will ever enjoy running and other physical activity. When I was young I also saw how happy it made my dad to watch me make friends and sometimes push other girls over. When I started playing it also made my mom super excited she got this bumper sticker "not your average soccer mom" and put it on the trunk of her mini van. Ever since I was little I used it as a release and knew it was a way to get adrenaline, serotonin, and dopamine all coursing through my body. Even now a complete 12 years later and I still use it as a cathartic release. Although now that im in college still playing competatively its more than just a hobbie to me. This pandemic has cancelled our regular season so its been extremely hard for all of us because we should practicing every day, having three games every week, and traveling hours away together but instead we barely get any time on the field in groups of 4. It’s been hard but I know I would take an hour on the field with a couple girls over nothing at all everytime. This sport has introduced me to some of my best friends and has really shaped me to who I am today.

Surfing

Being from a coastal town surfing was just a part of growing up and it has made me feel extrememly blessed because I had that opportunity. My father went to San Diego State University simply so he could contnue surfing and now he is turning 69 still waking up every morning at 5 am to surf with his buddies and he loves every bit of it. He use to manage a surf shop called “Doyle Sports,” he ran it with his business partner Mike Doyle. Since surfing was so huge in his life he obviously had to teach me and my sister how to surf and love every part of it. He would push us into waves on our foam wavestorm boards and when we would fall off he’d make sure to reassure us and say it happens to everyone even the professionals so we shouldn’t be discouraged to stop just because were afraid of wipping out. I’ve continued surfing during the summer with friends and alone because its a super fun social activity but also extrememly relaxing. It’s a hobbie that I know im not the best at but always see improvement everytime I do it. It’s mostly an activity close to my heart that brings me satisfaction and peace of mind.

Painting

I only took up painting because my sister is an artist and she found so much joy in drawing and art that I thought I could try to do it too. I love painitng abstract obejcts and people. My sister is also my biggest critique and supporter when it comes to art which personally I love. I’ve recently tried to paint simple form figures and landscapes. Its all about coming out of my comfort zone since im not really good at that but taking up painting as a hobbie has taught me that. This new hobbie of mine has taught me some pretty valuable lessons mostly revolving around my patience and happy mistakes.

Reading Tarot Cards

Reading tarot cards is definitely my most recent hobbie. My neighbor had gotten me really into it during quaratine. I invested in my own deck and now give all my friends readings. The way it works is you ask a pretty broad question and then read the cards in the spots they are placed. I always read my cards in the form of a celtic cross where every position has a different way to read the card. For exmaple the first card down is that person in the present, theres a card laid on top and thats their challenge theyre facing. One card on the back is the past, in front is the present, above is their strengths, below is subconcious and what its trying to tell you. On the side of that formation theres four cards. First is your sugessted path, second what you need to know, third is hopes and fears and fourth is your outcome card. Now there are two types of cards: Major Arcana cards and Minor Arcana cards. Major arcana contain the sun, moon, fool, devil, and more. While the Minor Arcana contains 4 suits the wands, swords, pentacles, and cups with a 1 through 10, queen, king, knight, and page in each suit. The wands represent your energy, motivation, passion, spirituality, and strengths. The swords are your thoughts, words, and actions. The pentacles are your finances, work, and material posessions. Finally the cups are your feelings, emotions, intuition, and creativity. Giving people readings makes me feel happy because it helps them understand situations they’re in or see it from another perspective.
